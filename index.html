About Me

My name is Nicole Apsche, and I live in West Philadelphia. I moved to Philadelphia in 2012 to attend Temple Univeristy, where I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Neuroscience in 2016. I love animals, food, running, traveling, being outside, and spending time with friends. I am hard working and reliable, and work well alone and with a team.

I worked as an Account Executive for KGTiger, a talent acquisition company based out of Bala Cynwyd, PA. I was displaced due to COVID at the end of May, 2020, which gave me an opportunity to really focus and pour all of my energy into the Full Stack Certification Course at the University of Pennsylvania. Before starting this course, I did not even own a computer. I am really proud of myself for how far I have come in the past 24 weeks. I am excited to continue learning more programming languages and beginning my career as a developer.
