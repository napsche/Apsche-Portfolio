Git-Pets 2.0

Git-Pets 2.0 was created as an updated version to the original version, Git-A-Pet. Both were designed to be a user-friendly platform for people to search for adoptable animals. Espeically with the pandemic, many people are now working remotely permanently, and are looking for some furry coworkers! We migrated the application to React, and used MongoDB as our database instead of MySQL, which was an especially difficult tasks but a huge learning experience. During this project, the other team members fell into some personal issues, so I became the lead developer on this project. I developed every aspect of this project, and overcame a lot of challenges along the way. I am extremely proud of how my skills improved while building this application, and I look forward to continuing development on this in the future. Created by Nicole Apsche, Margaret Egner, and Solomon Lee.

Technologies Used

HTML, CSS, React, Bootstrap 4, PetFinder API, MongoDB, Mongoose, PassportJS, Bcrypt
